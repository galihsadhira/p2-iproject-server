## Endpoints

List of Available Endpoints:

Products
- `POST /products`
- `GET /products`
- `GET /products/:id`
- `PUT /products/:id`
- `DELETE /products/:id`

Categories
- `POST /categories`
- `GET /categories`
- `GET /categories/:id`
- `PUT /categories/:id`

Ingredients
- `POST /ingredients`
- `GET /ingredients`
- `GET /ingredients/:id`
- `PUT /ingredients/:id`
- `DELETE /ingredients/:id`

Recipes
- `POST /recipes`
- `GET /recipes`
- `GET /recipes/:id`
- `PUT /recipes/:id`
- `DELETE /recipes/:id`

Sales
- `POST /sales`
- `GET /sales`
- `GET /sales/:id`
- `PUT /sales/:id`
- `DELETE /sales/:id`
